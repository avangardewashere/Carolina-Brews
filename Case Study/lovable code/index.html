Rooted in
Carolina Culture.

Carolina Brews started in 2018 with a simple mission: bring world-class coffee to the Carolinas while honoring our local roots.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms across Central and South America, then roast them by hand in small batches right here in Charlotte. Every bag tells a story of craft, care, and community.
